08 / The journey

Roadmap

From the first line of code to a twenty-one-app universe. Here's the flight plan — 🚀 marks where we are right now.

✓

Late 2025

The ideaDone

Two ecosystems sketched out: an AI toolkit that actually helps, and a suite built for Islamic learning and community life.

✓

Jan 2026

First lines of codeDone

Soar and Sirat repos created. Lots of false starts, a lot of deleted code, and the first real architecture.

✓

Apr 2026

Soar AI alphaDone

The first assistant flows come together — chat, tasks, and daily briefings, running end to end for the first time.

🚀

Jul 2026 — You are here

Building nowBuilding

Soar AI, Soar Work, and Soar Studio are all actively in progress, alongside Sirat's Foundation and Learning pillars — and SILA has quietly entered formation as a third system.

○

Late 2026

First public betasPlanned

Soar Work and Sirat Quran open up to early testers — the first apps real people outside my circle get to use.

○

Early 2027

SILA comes into viewPlanned

The third system leaves formation and gets its own real teaser — what binds it together starts to come out of the dark.

○

Mid 2027

Developer access opensPlanned

A public API, a Vanguard tier, and a direct line to the roadmap — the first people building on top of Soar and Sirat, not just inside them.

○

2027

Soar Business + Sirat SchoolsPlanned

The business operating system and the school management layer come online, extending both ecosystems outward.

✦

2027 & beyond

Three systems, one universeVision

Soar, Sirat, and SILA, fully connected — every app, every pillar, live and talking to each other.
