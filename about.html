Origin

Where I'm from

I was born in Dhaka, Bangladesh and now live in Irving, Texas. That mix of cultures — the energy of Dhaka and the space of Texas — shapes how I see technology and what I want to build with it.

I kept running into the same problem: the tools that existed almost did what I needed — just not quite. So I started sketching what I actually wanted, and then I started building it.

Soar came first — an AI-powered toolkit for the way I actually think and work, instead of a dozen apps that don't talk to each other. Sirat grew out of wanting that same care and craft applied somewhere that mattered just as much: Islamic learning and community life. Software for the deen deserves to be as good as everything else.

I'm still early. Both ecosystems are being built one app at a time — a lot of sketching, rebuilding, and starting over. That's honestly the part I like most.
